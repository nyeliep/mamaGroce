How it works



Browse products


Navigate to the browsing section, choose a category, scroll through the products or use the search bar, click on a product to see more details, add the product to your cart if you would like to purchase it, and continue browsing or proceed to checkout when ready

Add to Cart


To add items to your cart, click on the product and click the "Add to Cart" button, adjust the quantity if necessary, continue shopping, review your items and make any changes if needed, and proceed to checkout by providing your payment and delivery information.

Checkout and pay


To check out,you need to review your cart, choose your delivery option, enter your delivery address, review your order and make any changes if necessary, enter your payment information, review your payment details, and submit your order.
